White Bengal Tiger

They have blue eyes that make them look exotic and are actually white due to a recessive trait and not albinism.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris Tigris
  • Average Length:2.5 - 3 meters (female - male)
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Forests, Mangrove Swamps, and moist jungles

Once thought to be an entirely different subspecies, the White Bengal Tiger has gained lots of popularity. From its white coat to its brown or black stripes, as well as its majestic icy blue stare; these tigers tend to grow faster and be larger than their fiery orange counterparts
